|Overlay|.1 Design Issues
-----------------------------------------------

Just as the Internet was once a new networking technology developed as
an overlay on the telephone network, overlays have frequently been
used to test out new networking ideas on top of the Internet. The
MBone (multicast backbone) was one early overlay that enabled
experimentation with IP multicast routing and forwarding. Similarly,
the 6Bone was used in the early days of IPv6 to experiment with IPv6
routing and forwarding using an overlay on top of the IPv4
Internet. Both IP muliticast and IPv6 were then implemented in
commercial routers, but only IPv6 made it into widespread deployment
in the Internet (and even that took a couple of decades).
